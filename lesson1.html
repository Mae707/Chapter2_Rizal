Family Background & Siblings

Family Background

Jose Rizal was born in Calamba, Laguna to Francisco Mercado and Teodora Alonso Realonda. His family belonged to the principalia class and lived through honest labor and education.

Siblings

Rizal had 10 siblings: Saturnina, Paciano, Narcisa, Olympia, Lucia, Maria, Concepcion, Josefa, Trinidad, and Soledad. Each played a role in shaping his upbringing and values.
